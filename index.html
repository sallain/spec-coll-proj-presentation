We have amazing collections

And we have researchers who want to access them online

But we also have budgets


And staffing shortages and cataloguing backlogs and software licenses and
CCPERB applications and hardware breakdowns and ...

How do we make great digital projects with a finite amount of resources?

Leverage what we already have

When we catalogue:

  • Create good MARC records
  • Automate MARCXML export from the catalogue
  • Transform our good MARC automagically
  • Pollinate the catalogue with links to digital content

Collaborate and listen

When we develop infrastructure:

  • Build our tools together
  • Make our systems interoperable (in and out of the university)
  • Share our training and experiential learning
  • Contribute back to the community
